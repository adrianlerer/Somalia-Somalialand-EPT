## 9. ALTERNATIVE EXPLANATIONS ASSESSMENT

### 9.1 "Leadership Quality" Hypothesis

**Alternative**: Somalilandia just had **better leaders** (Egal, Riyale, Silanyo) vs. Somalia (Abdullahi Yusuf, Sheikh Sharif, Farmajo).

**Counter-Evidence**:
1. **Institutional Constraints**: Somalilandia leaders also tried term extensions (Riyale 2008, Bihi 2022) but **institutions checked** them (Supreme Court 2023)

**Assessment**: ‚ùå Leadership quality is **ENDOGENOUS** to CLI (rigid institutions select for or constrain leaders).

### 9.2 "Colonial Legacy" Hypothesis

**Assessment**: ‚ùå Colonial legacy was **neutralized** by 31 years of unified governance. Divergence stems from **1991-2001 formative decade**, not 1884-1960 colonialism.
