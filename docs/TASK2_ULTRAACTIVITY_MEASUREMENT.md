## 1. METHODOLOGY

### Ultraactivity Score Calculation (UA)

**Formula**: UA = (TE + PP + FP) / 3

Where:
- **TE (Term Extensions)**: Ratio of actual term length to constitutional term
  - 0.0 = all transitions on schedule
  - 1.0 = systematic term extensions beyond limits
  
- **PP (Political Persistence)**: Persistence of temporary political arrangements
  - 0.0 = all temporary arrangements expire
  - 1.0 = all temporary arrangements become permanent
  
- **FP (Formula Persistence)**: Longevity of power-sharing formulas
  - 0.0 = formulas renegotiated regularly
  - 1.0 = formulas operate indefinitely without modification

**Interpretation**:
- UA < 0.40: LOW ultraactivity (flexible institutions)
- UA 0.40-0.60: MODERATE ultraactivity
- UA > 0.60: HIGH ultraactivity (ossified institutions)
