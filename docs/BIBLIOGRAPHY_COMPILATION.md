## CHICAGO 17TH EDITION FORMATTING NOTES

**Author-Date Style Requirements**:
1. **In-text citations**: (Dawkins 1982, 112) or Dawkins (1982)
2. **Bibliography entries**: Author Last, First. Year. *Title*. Place: Publisher.
3. **Journal articles**: Author. Year. "Title." *Journal* Volume (Issue): Pages. DOI.
4. **Edited volumes**: Author. Year. "Chapter." In *Book Title*, edited by Editor, pages. Place: Publisher.
5. **Reports**: Organization. Year. "Report Title." Place: Publisher. URL.
6. **News articles**: Outlet. Year. "Headline." Date. URL.

**Special Cases**:
- **UN documents**: Use S/YEAR/NUMBER format in citation
- **Government reports**: Cite issuing ministry/agency as author
- **Constitutional texts**: Cite as government document with adoption date
- **Working papers**: Include SSRN or repository URL
